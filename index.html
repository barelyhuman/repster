Repster

A simple 3 Set Rep Generator to increase your single set Reps count


    Rest Timer


    How To Use?

    Test your max

    Enter the maximum repitions you can do in one single set without resting. Make sure you test this each week to see if there was an improvement

    The Rules

    Know, that the count is just a simple measurement and shouldn't be used to measure strength but instead to measure how many reps you can do with proper form

    • Rest between sets (min:30 seconds, max: 60 seconds)
    • If you cannot do the exercise in proper form anymore, stop and take rest.
